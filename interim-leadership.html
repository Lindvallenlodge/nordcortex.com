Interim Leadership

We step in as accountable leaders for Procurement, Production/Manufacturing, Product Development, and Supply Chain — stabilising operations fast, rebuilding clarity, and delivering measurable outcomes while capability is built for the long term.

Outcomes We Own

  • Cost — fact‑based category savings (5–12%) via open‑costing and total landed‑cost visibility.
  • Speed — clean specs, decision rights, and cadence to remove drift and shorten lead times.
  • Quality — right‑first‑time methods and supplier routines to reduce rework and variability.
  • Control — live KPIs, supplier performance, and tier transparency embedded into governance.

Where We Step In

  • Procurement — category & sourcing strategy, should‑cost, RFx leadership, negotiation and contracts.
  • Production / Manufacturing — industrial engineering reviews, CAPEX‑neutral automation, packaging engineering and labelling.
  • Product Development — specifications/BOM governance, testing & compliance, right‑first‑time routines.
  • Supply Chain — S&OP enablement, DC→store flow, allocation basics, cost‑to‑serve visibility.
  • RFID / DPP (as enablers) — business case, source‑tagging, DC/retail integration, pilot→scale, data model & analytics handover.

How We Work

  • Lead → deliver → enable — results now, capability built for later.
  • Clear priorities with weekly results tracking — no noise, no drift.
  • Direct access to decision‑makers — transparency and speed.
  • Handover that secures continuity after the assignment ends.

First 30 / 60 / 90

  • Day 1–30: regain control — ownership, escalation paths, and a working cadence. Stabilise risks and unblock decisions.
  • Day 31–60: fix the core — clean specs/BOMs, cost models, KPIs, supplier routines, and governance.
  • Day 61–90: scale — embed operating model, automate the visible, and stand up dashboards & handover packs.

Deliverables You Get

  • Category playbooks & sourcing calendars.
  • Open‑cost models + total landed‑cost views.
  • Supplier scorecards & contract/KPI packs.
  • Factory assessment notes & CAPEX‑neutral automation roadmap.
  • RFID/DPP rollout plan, data spec, and dashboards.
  • Operating cadence, roles, and clean handover documentation.
