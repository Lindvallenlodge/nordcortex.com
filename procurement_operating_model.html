Procurement Operating Model

One unified system linking Commercial / Product, Product Enabling & Traceability, and Indirect sourcing streams into a single, disciplined procurement engine. Focus on tier mapping, open costing, RFX excellence, and fact-based negotiation.

Tier 1‑2‑3 visibility Open costing RFI/RFQ/RFP Supplier performance Governance

Procurement System Core

Tier Mapping (1‑2‑3)

Visibility from final assembler to raw materials.

  • Map all direct and indirect suppliers per BOM item
  • Link traceability to DPP and compliance fields
  • Risk heatmap by dependency, geography, capacity
  • Enable proactive dual-sourcing and scenario planning

Open Costing Model

Fact‑based structure aligning both sides of the table.

  • Transparent BOM: materials, labour, overhead, margin
  • Factory efficiency factors and yield tracking
  • Conversion cost analysis and local benchmarks
  • Pack & label separated to show packaging true cost

RFX Excellence

Structured sourcing steps ensuring transparency and speed.

  • RFI: capability & compliance validation
  • RFQ: cost and service comparison with volume brackets
  • RFP: holistic value evaluation and award criteria
  • Negotiation dashboards connected to open costing sheets

Negotiation & Award

Execution logic combining analytics with supplier relationship.

  • Target-cost alignment to strategic category curve
  • Fact-based trade‑offs (MOQ, payment, tooling)
  • Dual‑source or backup structure for continuity
  • Signed annexes linking spec, tests, artwork, DPP

Supplier Development & SRM

Continuous improvement via KPIs and reviews.

  • Monthly scorecards: cost, quality, delivery, CO2
  • Quarterly business reviews and CI backlogs
  • Tiered SRM governance: strategic / tactical / transactional

Design → Source → Validate → Award → Operate

1 · Design
Define specs, costing logic, and supplier mapping.
2 · Source
RFI to shortlist → RFQ with breakdowns → negotiation loops.
3 · Validate
Sampling, testing, compliance checks before award.
4 · Award
Contracts, KPIs, change control, and supplier onboarding.
5 · Operate
Continuous monitoring and performance reviews.
