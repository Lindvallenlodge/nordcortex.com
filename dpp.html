Digital Product Passport — NordCortex Approach

Design once, capture at source, reuse everywhere. We build interoperable product passports that connect materials, manufacturing, logistics, retail, and recycling data — ready for EU DPP and practical business value today.

01 · Overview

What is a DPP?

  • Traceability & circularity passport attached to each product
  • Core data: origin, composition, certifications, care/repair, end‑of‑life
  • Works with RFID, NFC, QR, and IoT signals
Goaltrusted product data
