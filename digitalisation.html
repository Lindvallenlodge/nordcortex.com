Digitalisation for Retail & Manufacturers

We help big retailers, medium retailers, and large manufacturers move from paper-and-spreadsheet processes to measurable digital flows — sourced from the line, the DC, and the store — with clean IDs, read‑points, and exception playbooks.

Single source of truth

  • Item/lot/serial IDs aligned across ERP, WMS, POS
  • Source tagging + backroom & salesfloor read‑points
  • Exception playbooks: shrink, mispick, dwell
