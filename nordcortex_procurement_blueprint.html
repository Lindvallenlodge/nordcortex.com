NordCortex Procurement Blueprint

An industry‑agnostic framework built around 10 core pillars of procurement excellence — connecting strategy, sourcing, cost transparency, and supplier performance into one operating model.

01 · Strategy

Strategy & Governance

  • Category strategies aligned with business goals
  • Supplier segmentation, sustainability integration
  • Procurement policy, authority matrix, and cadence
02 · Spend

Spend & Category Management

  • Spend cube and demand control
  • Pipeline prioritization and benchmarking
  • Category maturity and Kraljic analysis
03 · RFX

Sourcing & RFX

  • Structured RFI‑RFQ‑RFP processes
  • Evaluation scoring and supplier onboarding
  • e‑Auctions and compliance checks
04 · Cost

Costing & Total Cost of Ownership

  • Should‑cost and cost breakdown modeling
  • TCO and scenario analysis
  • FX, inflation, and value engineering
05 · Contracts

Negotiation & Contracting

  • BATNA/ZOPA playbooks
  • SLAs, KPIs, change control, CLM
  • Signature workflows and version control
06 · SRM

Supplier Relationship Management

  • Segmentation by strategic importance
  • Scorecards and quarterly reviews
  • Innovation and improvement tracking
07 · P2P

Operational Procurement (P2P)

  • PR‑PO‑GR‑Invoice workflow
  • Catalogs and guided buying
  • Supplier data governance and payment terms
08 · Risk

Risk & Compliance

  • Risk register and continuity plans
  • Quality, social, and cyber audits
  • ESG and regulatory compliance
09 · Digital

Data, Systems & Tools

  • ERP, e‑Sourcing, CLM, SRM tools
  • Spend analytics and dashboards
  • AI‑based opportunity and contract mining
10 · People

Organisation & Performance

  • Central vs local models
  • Skills matrix and capability programs
  • KPIs and stakeholder engagement
