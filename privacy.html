Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-11-06

NordCortex AB (“NordCortex”, “we”, “us”) is a business development & innovation advisory based in Stockholm, Sweden. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling personal data responsibly.

How we use data

Legal bases

We process personal data on the basis of legitimate interest (to run and protect our business) and/or contract (to respond to and manage your request).

Retention

We keep personal data only as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected, or as required by law.

Sharing

We may share limited data with trusted service providers (e.g., secure hosting and email) under data processing agreements. We do not allow providers to use data for their own purposes.

International transfers

Our service providers may operate in the EU/EEA and other jurisdictions with appropriate safeguards in place (e.g., SCCs).

Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you can request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal data. Contact us at info@nordcortex.com.
