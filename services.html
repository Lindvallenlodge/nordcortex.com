Services

Procurement Strategy & Category Management

  • End-to-end category leadership – sourcing strategy, supplier mapping, cost & risk optimization.
  • Operating model setup – governance, RASIC roles, KPI hierarchy, category playbooks.
  • Benchmarking & should-cost models – data-based cost structures, negotiation support, and savings tracking.
  • Procurement digitalization – system implementation, analytics dashboards, spend transparency.
  • Procurement capability training – coaching buyers on fact-based negotiations, open-costing, and supplier evaluation.

Supplier Development & Open-Costing

  • Open-costing frameworks – full transparency from raw materials to logistics using standardized cost breakdowns.
  • Supplier maturity programs – process mapping, line efficiency audits, improvement roadmaps.
  • Technical capability enhancement – lean implementation, automation alignment, and quality gate systems.
  • Strategic partnership models – co-creation projects, JV structures, and performance-linked incentives.
  • Supplier onboarding due diligence – compliance, capacity, financial, and technical scoring systems.

Product Development & Industrialization

  • Concept-to-bulk standardization – connecting PD, design, and manufacturing through structured routines.
  • Tech pack and BOM accuracy – version control, traceability, and feedback loops.
  • Material optimization – specification harmonization and alternative sourcing for cost and sustainability gains.
  • Automation and digital sampling – 3D design, virtual approvals, CAD integration.
  • Production feasibility studies – factory readiness, pilot run evaluation, yield and cycle time optimization.

RFID, IoT & Digital Product Passport

  • RFID source-tagging programs – rollout design, read-point mapping, packaging integration.
  • Digital Product Passport (DPP) – building data architecture for traceability, recyclability, and reporting.
  • IoT-driven inventory systems – linking production, logistics, and retail visibility in real-time.
  • Battery-free Bluetooth (BFBT) pilots – IoT sensor deployment for environment & location tracking.
  • Data utilization frameworks – integrating supply and retail data for forecasting, replenishment, and demand sensing.

Packaging, Logistics & Automation

  • Transit packaging optimization – right-sizing, material substitution, cost per unit modeling.
  • Automation audits – ROI and payback models for packing, folding, and labeling.
  • Vendor packaging support – global packaging guidelines, 3D pack optimization.
  • DC integration – carton scanning, content verification, and gate-reader design.
  • Environmental packaging – lifecycle analysis, recyclability and recycled content standards.

Quality, Compliance & Sustainability

  • QA systems and governance – standardized routines for TCs, CoCs, and AQL testing.
  • Root cause & CAPA frameworks – preventive and corrective actions linked to vendor scorecards.
  • Social & environmental compliance – managing BSCI, BEPI, OCS, SMETA, RCS, and FSC certification.
  • Traceability architecture – factory-to-store mapping for material flow and environmental footprint.
  • Sustainability dashboards – tracking compliance, CO₂, and recycling rates.

Supply Chain Optimization

  • Network redesign & nearshoring evaluation – cost, CO₂, and service level modeling.
  • S&OP setup – demand planning, parameter calibration, and alignment between PD and logistics.
  • Digital planning tools – linking ERP, WMS, and PLM to real-time data layers.
  • Lead-time compression – milestone automation, calendar redesign.
  • Inventory health improvement – visibility, obsolescence control, and predictive replenishment.

Process & Change Management

  • Process architecture & SOP creation – connecting PD, procurement, production, and sustainability flows.
  • Change enablement – communication and training programs for adoption.
  • Performance dashboards – real-time KPI tracking and escalations.
  • RACI & governance models – clear accountability between HQ and production offices.
  • Continuous improvement culture – agile routines aligned to business rhythm.

Interim & Advisory Roles (B2B)

  • Interim leadership – Head of Procurement, Head of Production, or RFID/DPP Program Lead (3–12 months).
  • Operational turnaround projects – stabilizing operations post-M&A or supplier failure.
  • Board-level advisory – category, cost, and supply chain due diligence for investors.
  • Procurement and supply audits – red/amber/green diagnostics with 90-day improvement plans.
  • Innovation scouting – identifying automation, digital, or material innovation opportunities.

Commercial Enablement & Training

  • RFP & contract playbooks – templates, scoring matrices, and risk clauses.
  • Negotiation capability programs – analytics-driven training and supplier performance improvement.
  • Digital learning tools – modular e-learning for factories and sourcing offices.
  • Vendor and buyer certification – benchmarking and continuous improvement frameworks.

Added Services

  • Tech stack consulting – PLM, WMS, RFID middleware, or ERP integration advisory.
  • Data analytics & KPI visualization – Power BI and Looker Studio dashboards.
  • Sustainability data collection – automated EU/ESG reporting pipelines.
  • Cross-functional project PMO – program management for transformation projects.
  • Audit-ready documentation setup – compliance, TC/CoC, and product data capture standardization.
